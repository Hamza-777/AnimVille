One Piece Filler List


One Piece is an ongoing anime series that started in 1999. So far 961 episodes of One Piece have been aired. With a total of 99 reported filler episodes, One Piece has a very low filler percentage of 10%.

One Piece is the story of Monkey D. Luffy who became a rubber man after accidently eating a Devil Fruit. In his quest, Luffy builds his crew and continues on his adventure to find the mysterious treasure One Piece.

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 1997, with its individual chapters compiled into 97 tankōbon volumes as of September 2020.

Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- is an ongoing anime series that started in 2016. So far 38 episodes of Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- have been aired. Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- has no reported filler.

Natsuki Subaru, an ordinary student, is on his way home from the convenience store when he finds himself transported to another world. As he's lost and confused in a new world where he doesn't even know left from right, the only person to reach out to him was a beautiful girl with silver hair. Determined to repay her somehow for saving him from despair, Subaru agrees to help the girl find something she's looking for...

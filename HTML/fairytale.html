Fairy Tail Filler List


Fairy Tail was an anime series that ran from 2009 to 2019. In total 328 episodes of Fairy Tail were aired. With a total of 63 reported filler episodes, Fairy Tail has a low filler percentage of 19%.

The story follows Lucy Heartfilla who is determined to join the notorious magical Fairy Tail Guild. During a daring rescue, she encounters Natsu who is part of the guild and eventually offers her a place. They become teammates performing various missions for the Fairy Tail Guild. Be prepared for an action-packed adventure!
